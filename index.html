After creating an account, head to the "Create New Campaign" button above.

Enter a unique campaign ID, a name for your epic adventure, where it all began, and the user IDs of the players in your campaign separated by spaces.

After creating an account, head to the "My Campaigns" button above to see every campaign you're a part of.

Been a while since last session, and can't quite remember who that one guy was? Read up on characters before your next brawl!

The "Sessions" tab within a campaign lets you read up on what happened previously. You can save your GM some work and create Session notes yourself to share with your party.
