Total Sales, Profit, and Profit Ratio by Category

Office Supplies have the highest profit of the three categories, followed by Electronics, then Furniture.

Profit Ratio is in the negative for almost all three, Furniture is the only category without a negative Profit Ratio.

Electronics has the highest Sales Amount, interestingly, compared to its Profit.


Profit Trends Over Time By Region

The East region has a peak disproportionate to the other regions in September and October of 2023. The North region has the lowest profit of all regions in March of 2023.

All together, there seems to be a light seasonality where during the Spring, sales tend to go down, then the Fall is when profit is best.
